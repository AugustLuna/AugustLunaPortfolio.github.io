Mapping Methane Lakes

In this project, my goal was to turn integer data transmitted from a satalite, into a map of Titan's Methane Lakes. I created source code that mapped a Latitude and Longitude grid system to inset the data into, as well as learned to downsample.

Rising Temperatures in the Rio Grande Valley

In this project, I focused on using CMIP 6 earth system model data to construct climate graphs over the Rio Grande Valley. This was done to determine what affects global warming might have on its population and economy.
